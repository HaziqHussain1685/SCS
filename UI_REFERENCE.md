## Color Guide

### Group Colors
- рҹ”ө **Blue** (#3b82f6) - Default, general areas
- рҹҹЈ **Purple** (#8b5cf6) - Important areas
- рҹ”ҙ **Red** (#ef4444) - Critical areas
- рҹҹ  **Orange** (#f97316) - Warning zones
- рҹҹЎ **Yellow** (#eab308) - Medium priority
- рҹҹў **Green** (#10b981) - Low risk areas
- рҹ”· **Cyan** (#06b6d4) - Special zones

- рҹ”ҙ **Critical** (Health вүӨ 30) - Immediate action
- рҹҹ  **High** (Health вүӨ 60) - Urgent
- рҹҹЎ **Medium** (Health вүӨ 80) - Monitor
